CHAPTER 26

The Absolute

He stood.

Not returned.

Revealed.
The pressure spread.

Not outward—
inward.

Everything bent toward him.
He did not move.

Movement adjusted instead.
They pushed forward.

And found resistance
without force.
Distance stretched.

Progress vanished.

Effort meant nothing.
Time slipped.

Cause lost sequence.

Nothing aligned.
He watched.

Not curious.

Not engaged.
Their power reached him.

And ceased to exist.
Understanding arrived.

Too late.
Scale shifted.

Not in size—
in meaning.
Silence took control.

Even thought slowed.
This was no longer a battle.

It was conclusion.
