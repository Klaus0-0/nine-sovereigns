CHAPTER 23

Awakening of the Eight

Something shattered within them.

Not flesh.
Not will.

Limitation.
Authority reclaimed.
The first awakened strike landed.

Reality bent to accommodate it.

This was no longer power.
This was command.
Nihilus was struck.

Not wounded.
Not weakened.

He bled absence—
and the ground recoiled.
One Sovereign pressed forward.

Domain absolute.
Will unquestioned.

For a moment—
Nihilus yielded ground.
Another followed.

Power layered upon power.

The assault was no longer chaotic.
It was deliberate.
Nihilus adjusted.

Not retreating.
Not resisting.

Reconfiguring.
Efficient.
The Eight moved as one.

Domains aligned.
Timing perfected.

This was war—
not chaos.
For the first time,
Nihilus slowed.

Space resisted his passage.

The impossible had weight.
One Sovereign pushed too far.

Authority strained.
Identity wavered.

Power does not forgive excess.
The cost arrived immediately.

Domains flickered.
Control faltered.

Awakening demanded payment.
The battlefield quieted.

Power lingered in the air.

Nihilus still stood.
Changed—
but unbroken.
Momentum shifted.

Not toward victory.
Toward possibility.

The Eight sensed it.
Nihilus steadied himself.

Calm returned to his posture.

The Eight prepared to finish it.
Believing—
finally.
