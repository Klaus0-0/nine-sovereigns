CHAPTER 3

The Gift of Sovereignty

From the silence between worlds,
a figure emerged — neither god nor mortal.
A witness to collapse.
A bearer of fragments.
To the first king, he offered flame —
not as destruction,
but as will made manifest.
To the second, he gave stillness.
Ice that could halt even fate itself.
Lightning followed —
speed beyond thought,
judgment without mercy.
The earth answered next.
Endurance. Weight.
A crown that could never be set down.
Life was not gentle.
It demanded sacrifice —
and she accepted it.
Knowledge tore through flesh and soul alike.
Awareness became pain.
Shadow came last —
not as evil,
but as the truth light refuses to face.
Eight stood crowned.
Eight bore fragments of another’s soul.
And the witness… grew thinner with every breath.
