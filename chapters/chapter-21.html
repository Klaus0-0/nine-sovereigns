CHAPTER 21

Adaptation

The Eight regrouped.

Not to plan victory.
But to choose loss.

Power still answered.
Slower than before.
They struck together.

No restraint.
No caution.

Overwhelming force.
Absolute intent.
The attacks failed early.

Not resisted.
Not absorbed.

Reinterpreted.
Rewritten.
Nihilus did not counter.

He mirrored.

Their intent returned altered.
Optimized.

He was learning.
One Sovereign faltered.

Not wounded.
Not corrupted.

Their domain hesitated.
For the first time.
The universe reacted.

Stars dimmed.
Concepts blurred.

Mortals felt unease.
Without reason.
Nihilus was felt.

Not seen.
Not heard.

If he acted directly,
nothing would remain.
They retreated.

Not defeated.
Understood.

The void did not follow.
It no longer needed to.
