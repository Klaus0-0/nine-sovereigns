CHAPTER 29

The Execution

They moved.

Not to strike.

To construct.
Power flowed.

Not wild.

Directed.
Structure emerged.

Not natural.

Imposed.
He stood within it.

Watching.

Allowing.
The first seal locked.

Not force—
rule.
Pressure increased.

Space resisted.

Containment held.
They strained.

Not from weakness.

From scale.
He changed.

Not outwardly.

Fundamentally.
Another layer formed.

Deeper.

Stronger.
It began to break.

Not from him.

From them.
They held it together.

Refusing collapse.

Refusing failure.
It stabilized.

For a moment—

it worked.
Then—

something cracked.
