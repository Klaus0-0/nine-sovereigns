CHAPTER 27

The Cost of Existence

No one moved.

Not from fear.

From understanding.
One stepped back.

Not retreating.

Reconsidering.
Formation broke.

Unity fractured.

Silence grew heavier.
They tried again.

And nothing happened.

Not even resistance.
Nihilus remained.

Unaffected.

Uninvolved.
They turned on each other.

Not in anger.

In doubt.
One fell.

Not struck.

Empty.
None moved to help.

Not from cruelty.

From truth.
He watched.

The same as before.

The same as always.
Then—

something changed.

Not in him.
In them.
A new thought formed.

Fragile.

Dangerous.
This was not over.

But it would never be the same.
