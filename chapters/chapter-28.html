CHAPTER 28

The Plan

They gathered again.

Not as one.

But not apart.
One stepped forward.

Not to lead.

To propose.
An idea formed.

Not of victory.

Of survival.
They listened.

Not convinced.

But willing.
Not destruction.

Not defeat.

Containment.
The cost was clear.

Too clear.

Too great.
He remained.

Watching.

Waiting.
Decision settled.

Not unanimous.

But necessary.
Power returned.

Different.

Controlled.
Formation changed.

Not balance.

Strategy.
Silence returned.

Not empty.

Focused.
They faced him again.

Not to win.

To endure.
TO BE CONTINUED
