CHAPTER 2

The First Choice

Order returned to the world.
But peace did not.

Those who lived gathered in silence,
unsure of what came next.

One among them felt the imbalance.
Not fear. Responsibility.

“Why did it stop?”

“Because it had to.”

The first gift was not given to save the world—
but to let one person stand again.

He did not feel pain.

He did not feel relief.

He felt… less.

His choice did not go unseen.

He searched for relief.

Nothing answered.

The world would stand.

Whatever it cost him.
