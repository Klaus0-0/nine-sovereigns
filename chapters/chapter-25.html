CHAPTER 25

Nihilus Awakens

It ended.

Silence returned.

The universe breathed again.
He did not move.

No presence remained.

Only absence.
Then—

something shifted.

Barely.
The Eight lowered their guard.

Exhaustion replaced vigilance.

Victory settled in.
Something felt wrong.

Not danger.
Not threat.

Misalignment.
He rose.

Not with force.
Not with effort.

Naturally.
His eyes opened.

Not awakened—
aware.
Reality bent.

Not broken.

Corrected.
They stepped back.

Understanding returned—
too late.
Nihilus stood.

Unchanged.

Untouched.
This was not his fall.

This was transition.
The pressure returned.

Heavier.

Final.
They had not defeated him.

They had only prepared him.
