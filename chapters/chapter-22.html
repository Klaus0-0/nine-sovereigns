CHAPTER 22

Manifestation

The void did not rupture.

It parted.

Nihilus stepped forward.
Calm.
Complete.
The Eight faced him in silence.

No formation held.
No distance felt stable.

He stood alone.
That was enough.
His face held no malice.

No hunger.
No fury.

Only certainty—
old and exhausted.
They struck together.

Fire.
Storm.
Order.

Annihilation unleashed.
The impact consumed everything.

Light drowned shadow.
Force erased form.

For one moment—
hope existed.
The blast faded.

Nihilus remained.

Not shielded.
Not protected.

Present.
He walked forward.

Armor fractured—
not flesh, but meaning.

The ground recoiled beneath him.
A smile formed.

Barely.

Not triumph.
Not cruelty.

Confirmation.
He moved once.

Space forgot its rules.

Three Sovereigns vanished from place.
Not wounded—
displaced.
Shock rippled through the Eight.

This was not balance.
This was not contest.

This was permission withdrawn.
Nihilus stood unchanged.

Power bent around him.

He did not pursue.
He did not hurry.

Time adjusted instead.
Something broke within them.

Limits abandoned.
Domains expanded.

This was not corruption.
This was awakening.
Eight awakened Sovereigns stood ready.

Nihilus waited.

Unafraid.
Unmoved.

The real battle began here.
