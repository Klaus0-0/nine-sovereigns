CHAPTER 24

The Fall of Nihilus

They did not rush.

They rose.

For the first time—
he stood beneath them.
Alignment completed.

Not chaos.
Not force.

Structure imposed.
Space tightened.

The world narrowed.

For the first time—
resistance met him.
It began.

Not defeat.
Not collapse.

Fracture.
The strike landed.

Clean.
Precise.

Unavoidable.
The world broke.

Not around him—
through him.

Reality split open.
He dropped.

Not defeated.
Not finished.

Forced downward.
Silence replaced motion.

No smile remained.

Only focus.
Only awareness.
They bound him.

Not with chains—
but law.

Absolute.
Everything was released.

No restraint remained.

This was the end.
Light consumed all.

Form vanished.

Only force remained.
Silence returned.

The storm faded.

Only ruin remained.
He did not rise.

He did not move.

They believed it was over.
